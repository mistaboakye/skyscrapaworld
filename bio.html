BIO MUSIC MAJOR SKY SCRAPA VIDEO GRAFFITI

BIO

My birth name is Daniel Kwesi Mensah but my artist identity is Scrapa. I spent most of my childhood In Lagos, Nigeria but currently reside In Accra, Ghana. I was brought up in a lively entertaining home. I love to visit new places, explore, entertainment and have fun. I am an art-driven individual who is a graffiti artist, musician, songwriter, model, actor, and MC. To answer the question of who I am, I’d say I’m an embodiment of art.

Scrapa views his paintings as an articulation of variance in dealing with an element of Hip-Hop culture that is graffiti. With his visual medium, he uses Hip-Hop as subject matter and attempt to exploit its transcultural potential. His work appropriates and extends the aesthetics and iconography within this culture and usually takes the form of the murals or installations. Scrapa’s graffiti could be site-specific or displayed on walls, canvases, plywood, roofing sheets, and metals.
